About

Delfos

Delfos is an intelligent maintenance platform that optimizes the production of renewable energy assets. Through a SaaS platform Delfos provides owners and opperators failure prediction alerts and actionable insights.

Delfos is doing more than predict failures it is scaling up operational knowledge.

Throughout 2017 we have been monitoring approximately 300 wind turbines and providing our clients a evolving capability to retain operational knowledge, reduce operational costs and increase energy output.
