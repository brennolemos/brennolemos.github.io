About

Delfos is an intelligent maintenance platform that optimizes the production of renewable energy assets.

Through a SaaS platform Delfos provides owners and operators failure prediction alerts and actionable insights.

Features

Solutions

Delfos IM

It's an intelligent maintenance platform that integrates state of the art machine learning modules, and technical knowledge to transform the already available data from the wind turbine into useful information.

Delfos QI

It’s the mobile interface to Delfos platform. The app provides procedures, actionable insights and check-lists for operators and owners. Delfos IQ is the bridge connecting the Field Technician with Delfos’ Failure-Solution Database.

Benefits

Solutions

Avoid Unexpected Downtimes

Identify failure patterns before they evolve into unexpected failures. Delfos’ Failure-solution database provides the action plan to adress the failure pattern.

Optimize O&M Planning

With the online status of each asset, the operator can plan specific O&M actions individually, making preventive maintenance more effective.

Increase Asset Lifetime

By avoiding catastrophic failures and providing specific instructions for each asset, the lifetime and energy output are increased.

Increase Asset Performance

Monitor online the behaviour of every asset, underperformances can be treated as soon as they appear.
